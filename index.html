Let´s go to restaurant

Last Thursday our food experts team decided to have lunch in one of the most popular restaurants in Poblenou: La Terrassa (4.9/5 on Tripadvisor). After the disastrous Wednesday’s restaurant choice, they wanted to be on the safe side. Once they arrived in the restaurant, it was empty. Bad vibes. But they decided to give it a try. The menu was quite generous, like 10 main dishes and 10 more second dishes, including stews, salads, meat... and “fresh” fish (which Julie thought to be “cold” fish ). The price was affordable enough (11'95€ for the full menu - 7'95€ for half a menu). When the group thought their stomachs couldn’t make room for any more food, Julie’s stomach had a little room to try a traditional catalan dessert: ‘braç de gitano’ (in english: “gipsy’s arm”). With cream, sponge cake and LOTS of sugar. Safe bet, right? Well, she DIDN’T LIKE IT!! Of course french people really know about ‘cuisine’ and ‘pâtisserie’, so...
