Alanis Morissette in concert

Looks like amazing things are happening in Alanis Morissette's life since the "Ironic" singer started recording her 21st studio album (to which we still don't know the name). The best news to people from Barcelona is that Morissette is going to perform live and for free in ABC night club on Monday 13th. The only thing you need to do if you wanna come to the concert is registering in Alanis' website and booking your free tickets.
Also, don't forget that, after the concert, Alanis is gonna be in a meet&greet with the ones of you that have some questions for he about this next album she's recording. The place is still unknown, but it will be revealed at some point during the concert, so you fans can't miss it!

Walking dogs while skating

So... if walking your dog is fun, and skating is fun... can you imagine how much fun would it be walking your dog white skating? Now, stop imagining and do it! Every Wednesday, 7pm, come walk your dog with us and the rest of your neighbours. And remember that, after the walk, we're all gonna be chatting while having a drink (water for the animals) at the Dog get me wrong cafe.
